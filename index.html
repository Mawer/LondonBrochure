Waterloo Station

Take Southwest trains from Staines to London Waterloo, Day Travelcard required (journey time approx. 35mins)
Exit Waterloo onto the Southbank
Begin walking in the direction of Tower Bridge, opportunities for photos along the way

Southbank Walk

Sights include: Big Ben & Parliament, London Eye, St. Paul’s Cathedral, Shakespeare’s Globe Theatre, HMS Belfast, Tower Bridge & Tower of London
Optional stop at the Tate Modern for 360 degree views across London and coffee
Southwark Borough Market
Take tube, Jubilee Line, from London Bridge to Canary Wharf
Take DLR, from Canary Wharf to Cutty Sark

Greenwich

Light Lunch at 'The Green Cafe' in Greenwich
Explore Greenwich: Cutty Sark, see where time began, Greenwich market & boutique shops
Take DLR from Cutty Sark back to Canary Wharf
Take tube, Jubilee Line, from Canary Wharf to Westminster

Westminster

Closer look at Big Ben & Parliament, Westminster Abbey
Walk along Whitehall, passing 10 Downing Street
Walk through Horse Guards Parade into St. James’ Park
Walk through the park to join The Mall, continue up The Mall to Buckingham Palace

Knightsbridge

Walk along Constitutional Hill to Hyde Park Corner, continue along the road to Harrods
Explore Harrods with optional stop for Afternoon Tea
Take tube, Piccadilly Line, from Knightsbridge to Green Park
Take tube, Jubilee Line, from Green Park to Waterloo
Train back to Staines

NB. To follow this route takes approx. 8 hours in total, good walking shoes highly recommended.
Please be aware British weather is very unpredictable, dress appropriately
